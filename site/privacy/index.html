Privacy Policy

Atlas Arrows · Logan Land — Effective July 19, 2026

This policy explains what Atlas Arrows (“the app”, “we”), published by Logan Land — a studio based in Gyeonggi-do, Republic of Korea, serving players worldwide — collects when you play, why, and the choices you have. Questions? Write to support@loganland.app.

01 Playing without an account

You can install and play Atlas Arrows without creating an account or giving us your name. Your level progress is stored on your device by default.

02 Information we process

  • Optional sign-in. If you sign in with Apple or Google to sync progress or appear on leaderboards, we store a stable user identifier — not your password.
  • Game progress. Cleared stages and settings, kept on your device and, if you enable it, synced through your Apple iCloud or Google account.
  • Purchases. Hint packs and “remove ads” are processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play. We receive a confirmation of purchase — never your card number.
  • Advertising. We show ads through Google AdMob, which may process your device identifier, approximate IP-based region, and ad interactions to serve and measure ads.
  • Game services. Leaderboards and achievements use Apple Game Center / Google Play Games, governed by Apple's and Google's own privacy policies.
  • Notifications. Only if you allow them, your device's push token so we can send reminders.
  • Diagnostics. The app stores may share aggregate, non-identifying crash and performance data.

03 How we use it

To run and save your game, process purchases, show and measure ads, power leaderboards, respond to your support requests, and fix and improve the app. We do not use your data to build advertising profiles beyond what AdMob does under your consent.

04 Personalized ads & your consent

On iOS, personalized ads are shown only if you allow tracking through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt. In the EEA, UK, and Switzerland, we present a consent choice before serving personalized ads. You can change your mind at any time in your device settings, and reset your advertising identifier there too.

05 Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the service providers that make the app work — Google (AdMob, Play services), Apple (App Store, Game Center, iCloud) — and only as needed for those functions, or where the law requires.

06 Your choices & rights

  • Manage or withdraw ad-tracking consent in your device settings at any time.
  • Delete your account and associated data from within the app, or by emailing support@loganland.app.
  • Turn notifications on or off in your device settings.
  • Depending on where you live (e.g. GDPR, CCPA), you may request access to or deletion of your personal data — just contact us.

07 Children

Atlas Arrows is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. The app's store age rating reflects its content.

08 Retention, security & transfers

We keep data only as long as needed for the purposes above, then delete it. Purchases and sign-in identifiers are protected by the platforms that process them. Because our providers operate globally, your data may be processed in countries other than your own, under appropriate safeguards.

09 Changes

If we update this policy we'll revise the date above and, for significant changes, note it in the app. Continuing to play after an update means you accept the revised policy.
